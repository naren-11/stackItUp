ABOUT


Owl City is an American electronic project
created in 2007 in Owatonna, Minnesota;
it is one of several projects by singer,
songwriter and multi-instrumentalist Adam Young.
Young created the project while experimenting with
music in his parents' basement.
Owl City developed a following on the social
networking site MySpace, like many musicians who
achieved success in the late 2000s, before signing
with Universal Republic Records,in 2008.
